CSS writing-mode

The writing-mode CSS property defines whether lines of text are laid out horizontally or vertically and the direction in which blocks progress. This is essential for supporting internationalization, creative layouts, and advanced typographic effects.

  • Horizontal-tb (default): Text flows horizontally from left to right and lines stack top to bottom.
  • Vertical-rl: Text flows vertically from top to bottom and lines stack right to left.
  • Vertical-lr: Text flows vertically from top to bottom and lines stack left to right.
  • Sideways-rl / sideways-lr: Rotates text for creative vertical effects.

Syntax

writing-mode: horizontal-tb | vertical-rl | vertical-lr | sideways-rl | sideways-lr;
